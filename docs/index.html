The Story Behind Mkweli

From 25 years in NGO work to building free compliance tools for the organizations that need them most.

A Journey of Reskilling & Purpose

After 25 years working in NGOs across Africa, I witnessed firsthand how small organizations struggle with AML compliance. Commercial tools cost thousands per year‚Äîmoney that should go to the communities they serve.

When I decided to reskill into technology, I used free AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini) to teach myself programming. My first project was "GreenshieldAML"‚Äîbut the name was already trademarked.

So Mkweli was born. In Swahili, it means "honest" or "truthful"‚Äîthe perfect name for a compliance tool built on transparency and accessibility.

Mkweli is my gift back to the NGO community: professional-grade sanctions screening that any organization can deploy for free.
